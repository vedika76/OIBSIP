DR. APJ ABDUL KALAM

"You have to dream before your dreams can come true."

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, known as the “Missile Man of India” and the 11th President of the nation, was a visionary, scientist, and beloved leader. His humility, dedication, and inspiring words touched millions.

From humble beginnings to guiding India’s space and defense programs, Dr. Kalam proved that dreams, when backed by hard work, can transform into reality. He believed in empowering the youth and often said, “Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action.”

His legacy continues to inspire us to serve the nation with honesty, creativity, and compassion.
